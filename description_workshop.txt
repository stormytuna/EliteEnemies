Elite Enemies adds generic elite variations to enemies and (optionally) bosses!

[h2]TLDR[/h2]

- Adds 19 variations that can apply to any NPC
- Variations range from stat increases to unique effects
- Variations have rarities, which influence how often they appear and how much loot they drop
- Multiplayer is supported
- Currently supports English, see below if you'd like to contribute another translation

[h2]Configuration[/h2]

- Spawn chance of each rarity [0..1]
- Max simultaneous variations [0..20]
- Whether to apply to critters
- Whether to apply to bosses
